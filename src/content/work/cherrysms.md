I lead all technology and product at Cherry SMS, where I rebuilt a dormant platform from the ground up into a live product serving over 100 customers within months of launch.

- Architected a fully AWS CloudFormation-managed backend integrating Twilio and other APIs for account creation, phone provisioning, campaign management, and smart bulk messaging.

- Built and trained a custom PyTorch model on millions of messages to classify inbound replies and surface high-intent conversations.

- Designed the entire data layer on AWS Aurora PostgreSQL, with DynamoDB backing platform user data, and owned end-to-end security using AWS security groups and Secrets Manager.

- Engineered the system for high-volume messaging while staying compliant with carrier regulations.

- Built a private intranet for the team, self-hosted on EC2, with dashboards, data tooling, and n8n automation.

- Designed and built the full front end and back end, created the product's visual identity, authored the customer documentation, and manage the customer-service team.
